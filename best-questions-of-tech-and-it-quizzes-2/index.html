Best Questions of Tech and IT Quizzes #2

Sagar Khatri October 9, 2017 2017 Quiz Questions

Here we are presenting some of the Best questions from various Tech and IT Quizzes to help you prepare for IT quizzes like TCS IT Wiz (Article #2).

Best Questions and Answers of Tech and IT Quizzes #2

1. What does TFT stands for?
Ans: Thin Film Transistor

2. Who is the CEO of Intel Corporation?
Ans: Paul Otellini

3. What does VAIO stands for in SONY VAIO?
Ans: Video Audio Integration Operation

4. Who are the founders of Hewlett Packard?
Ans: Bill Hewlett and David Packard

5. Expand CDAC.
Ans: Centre for Development of Advanced Computing

6. Who is the CEO of E-Bay?
Ans: John Donahoe

7. Tell the code name of Android 3.0?
Ans: HoneyComb

8. Who is the CEO of AOL(American Online)?
Ans: Tim Armstrong

9. Which services do Apple devices use to synchronize data?
Ans: MobileMe

10. what does SGML stands for?
Ans: Standard Generalized Markup Language

11. Expand PERL.
Ans: Practical Extraction and Report Language

12. Expand VGA.
Ans: Video Graphics Array

13. Expand NASSCOM.
Ans: National Association of Software and Service Companies

14. Who is the co-founder of Apple along with Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak?
And: Ronald Wayne

15. Who is the first employee of Google?
Ans: Craig Silverstein

16. Who is the CEO of Twitter?
Ans: Dick Costolo

17. Who are the other co-founders of Twitter except Jack Dorsey?
Ans:  Evan Williams and Biz Stone

18. Who are the key people in Sun MicroSystems?
Ans: Andy Bechtolsheim, Jonathan Schwartz, Vinod Khosla, Bill Joy and Scott McNealy

19. Who are the key people in Infosys?
Ans: N.R.Narayan Murthy, Nandan Nilekani, Kris GopalKrishnan, Ashok Arora, K Dinesh and S.D.Shibulal, N.S.Raghavan

20. What is Blingtronics?
Ans: Wearable electronics made up of gold or diamonds.

21. Who is the present CEO of Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)?
Ans:  N. Chandrashekharan

22. Who is the only woman to achieve Alan Turing Award?
Ans: Barbara Liskov (Prof of CS in MIT)

23.TCS is the tech partner to the world’s oldest marathon. Which is it?
Ans: Boston Marathon

24. Expand TRDDC.
Ans: Tata Research Development and Design Centre

25. Who designed ENIAC?
Ans: John Mauchly and J.Presper Eckert

26. Who published the PASCAL programming language?
Ans: Niklaus Wirth in 1970

27. What is Object Pascal?
Ans: Derivative of Pascal for Object Oriented Programming

28. Who coined the term SiliconValley?
Ans: Ralph Vaerst and published by Don Hoefler

29. What is a Flog?
Ans: It is a Fake Blog

30. What is Napstering?
Ans: Distributing illegal content and making money out of it.

Hope you liked these one of the Best Questions of Tech and IT Quizzes. Stay tuned for more :)
