Important Questions for TCS IT Wiz 2017 Preparation

Sagar Khatri November 8, 2017 2017 Quiz Questions

Here we have some important questions for the preparation of TCS IT Wiz 2017. Prepare well for the quiz, and MTFBWY!

Make sure to read the questions and answers of TCS IT Wiz 2017 to get a preview of what may be asked for in the quiz.

1. Ever since Google launched a new version of Gmail, back in 2007, some people wondered why Gmails address includes a parameter called "shva“. https://mail.google.com/mail/u/0/?shva=1#inbox What is this “shva” ?

Ans: Should Have Valid Authentication

2. X is "an idea, behavior or style that spreads from person to person within a culture." X actsas a unit for carrying cultural ideas, symbols orpractices, which can be transmitted from one mind to another through writing, speech, gestures, rituals or otherimitable phenomena. Supporters of the concept regard X as cultural analogues to genes in that they self-replicate, mutate and respond to selective pressures. ID X.

Ans: Meme

3. X is a commonly used three-letter file name extension for text files that accompany various digital scene releases with information about them. Unlike README files, these files often contain elaborate ASCII art that nowadays can be viewed with dedicated X viewers, text editors with appropriate fonts or online in public X databases. ID X.

Ans: .nfo

Important Question for TCS IT Wiz: 

4. It is a plan of the Government of India to make allgovernment services available to the citizens of India via electronic media. This plan was an outcome of the recommendations of the second Administrative Reforms Commission. It isunder the administration of the Department of Information Technology of the Ministry of Communications and Information Technology. Identify.

Ans: National eGovernance Mission

5. This guy worked on designing rockets and guidance systems at Johns Hopkins University, then went on to join Fairchild Semiconductors, and eventually to co-found a company which is still refered to as one of the fairchildren. He is also famous for giving a law which now bears his name. Who am I talking about?

Ans: Gordon Moore

6. Unlimited Limited and Unincorporated Incorporated were two of the names this giant
considered giving itself before it took on its current name. Which company am I talking
about?

Ans: Microsoft

7. X was also ranked as the No. 1 for customer satisfaction within the first year of its launch with an overall score of over 95%. It have also been adjudged the 'Buzziest Brands of 2009' i.e. the most searched for brands by surfers, in a survey carried out by a leading online portal in India. ID X.

Ans: Virgin Mobile

8. Which popular voice and response client originated as an off shoot of the DARPA-funded CALO (Cognitive Assistant that Learns and Organizes) project?

Ans: Siri

9. In Superman Returns, Lex Luthor says that: "To the primitive mind, any sufﬁciently advancedtechnology would be indistinguishable from magic", the above is a paraphrase of whos quote?

Ans: Arthur Clarke

10. Steve Huffman and Alex Ohoniam are the founders of which social news and entertainment company?

Ans: Reddit

11. From time to time, in order to improve X’s usefulness for our users, X will send packets of data related to your usage of this product from a wireless transmitter to _____™, a heavily guarded, massively parallel server farm whose location is known only to …………, who carries its GPS coordinates on a 64-bitencrypted smart card locked in a stainless-steel briefcase handcuffed to his right wrist. No personally identifiable information of any kind related to your usage of X or any other current or future product will ever be given, sold, bartered, auctioned off, tossed into a late-night poker pot, or otherwise transferred in any
way to any untrustworthy third party, ever, we swear. See our Privacy Policy.”
Identify X.

Ans: Google Gulp

12. X is used by Google to enhance its search engine's search results with semanticsearch
information gathered from a wide variety of sources. X display was added to Google's search engine in 2012 starting in the United States, having been announced on May 16, 2012. It provides structured and detailed information about the topic in addition to a list of links to other sites. The goal is that users would be able to use this information to resolve their query without having to navigate to other sites and assemble the information themselves. ID X.

Ans: Google Knowledge Graph

13. The Princeton University statistician John W Tukey coined the term 'bit', an abbreviation of 'binary digit', and another very famous word which has become a part of everyday usage. Identify the word.

Ans: Software

14. This company was a division of Racal Electronics PLC in the early 1980s. In 1982, they started their business in UK. They launched themselves with their current name on January 1, 1985. Their logo is called the 'speechmark logo'. Name the company.

Ans: VodaFone

15. The name for this network administration utility comes from active sonar terminology. It operates by sending Internet Control Message Protocol packets to the target host and waiting for an ICMP response. In the process, it measures the round-­‐trip time. Identify.

Ans: Ping

16. Homonymous with "pal" or "partner" in Japanese, it incorporates emotions such as happiness and anger and instincts such as the need for companionship. It operates autonomously in response to external stimuli and its own temperament and express emotions as well. Equipped with adaptive learning and growth capabilities that allow each unit to develop a unique personality including behavior shaped by the praise and scolding of its owner. Identify it.

Ans: AIBO

17. The idea of this came to John Romero under the influence of Super Mario. The similarities are easily noticeable, such as the secret levels etc. Identify this famous game.

Ans: Dave

18. Which Indian developed news.google.com?

Ans: Krishna Bharat

19. Initially, the domain name for this product was intended to be Dorp.org (Dutch word for 'village'), but the creator made a typo and ended up with Drop.org. He liked it and the concept stuck on, albeit with a new name. In 2007, the creator started a commercial venture titled Acquia. What am I referring to?

Ans: Drupal

20. What is vampire power?

Ans: Power consumed by electronic devices like computers when they are in standby mode.
