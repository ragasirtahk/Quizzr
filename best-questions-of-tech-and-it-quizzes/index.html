The important things and tips to keep in mind before a tech or IT quiz like TCS IT Wiz are :-

1. Know about the quiz you are going for.

You should know well in advanced about the quiz you are going for, this will help you while preparing for the quiz. It is even better if you have had experience of the same quiz earlier too. 

2. Prepare well for the quiz.

Know all the important trivias based on tech and IT history. Every tech and IT quiz usually has atleast one question based on important incidents in the tech and IT world that occurred in the past.

3. Know the quizmaster and his style of questions.

You should know the quizmaster of the tech and IT quiz you are going for. This will help you prepare according to the style of the questions asked by that particular quizmaster.

For example, PickBrain in TCS IT Wiz asks mainly short answer questions unlike the long trivia based questions. So, you should focus on such questions while preparing for TCS IT Wiz.

4. Never leave an answer blank.

A common mistake done by new quizzers is that they leave an answer blank if they aren't able to answer the question. 

Most tech and IT quizzes have no negative marking so why leave an answer blank? Try identifying keyword in the trivia and link them. Several times you will find them related to a particular company, so simply follow this rule and fill up that company's name.

5. Read questions calmly and carefully.

Some newbie quizzers faint at the sight of trivia (pun intended). But seriously, several quizzers don't like reading the long trivial questions. But believe me, the trivial questions are one of the easiest one. They have several 'clues' hidden pointing towards the answer. Just read a trivia question carefully and you will be able to answer it.

6. Read questions from the previous iterations of the quiz.

 Always search for internet archives of the quiz you are participating in. Reading questions from the previous iterations of a quiz will help you understand the quiz and prepare accordingly. Follow blogs for this (you are already doing ;-)) like Quizzr.

For example, if you are participating in TCS IT Wiz, then you should take a look at the previous year questions of TCS IT Wiz. TCS IT Wiz 2017 Questions and Answers. TCS IT Wiz Questions and Answers.

7. Do not be afraid of tivial questions.

Some newbie quizzers faint at the sight of trivia (pun intended). But seriously, several quizzers don't like reading the long trivial questions. But believe me, the trivial questions are one of the easiest one. They have several 'clues' hidden pointing towards the answer. Just read a trivia question carefully and you will be able to answer it.

8. Know the difficulty level of the quiz.

If you know the level of the tech and IT quiz you are participating in then you will probably know the chances of qualifying in the finals after you attempt the prelims.

For example, the average cut-off of TCS IT Wiz is around 16-18, so if you are scoring around this in the TCS IT Wiz prelims than you know that there are high chances of you qualifying for the TCS IT Wiz finals.

9. Identify recent buzz and try making some questions.

Follow tech news blogs like TechCrunch, TheNextWeb to keep yourself aware about happenings in the world of tech and IT. If you are well aware of the recent happenings in the world of tech, then you can quite predict the questions of even big quizzes like TCS IT Wiz.

10. Follow latest tech and IT quiz blogs.

Hey, you are doing this already, aren't you? ;-) Keep following up to date tech and IT quiz blogs like Quizzr.
