About TCS IT Wiz:

TCS IT Wiz, a 'knowledge Initiative' started in 1999, is the biggest inter-school IT Quiz in India for students of class 8-12. The quiz aims to build awareness and emphasize the importance of IT skills, and enable students to look at technology through an innovative perspective.


The quiz platform works as an effective supplement to the computer science and IT field, as most of the questions researched for the quiz are beyond the academic areas, compelling the students to look beyond the text books.


As a pioneer in the field of IT quizzing, TCS IT Wiz has brought a paradigm shift in the world of quizzing with a mix of intelligent and interesting questions, and introduced new technology concepts such as software based rounds, gamification and animation.
Today, TCS IT Wiz has become a national benchmark in the inter-school quizzing circuit for the level of research, the intensity with which teams compete and the sheer participation it draws. The quiz has received immense patronage among the student community, teachers, parents and educational institutions and has become a part of the agenda for schools across the country.

TCS i.e. Tata Consultancy Services is a famous company.

How to prepare for TCS IT Wiz
TCS IT Wiz 2017 will be held in 12 cities across India - Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, Bhubaneswar, Chennai, Delhi, Hyderabad, Indore, Kolkata, Kochi, Mumbai, Nagpur and Pune.


The Regional Rounds culminate in a National Finals to be held in December 2017.


Entry & Eligibility:



There is NO entry or registration fee.

How to win TCS IT Wiz
Students from class 8 to 12 are eligible to participate.


Multiple teams per school (2 members per team) will be allowed to participate.

Ground Rules:



Participants should be present at the venue on the day of the event (refer www.tcsitwiz.com for the Calendar) at the specified time in their school uniform, with an official Id’s from the school, if any and carry their own lunch box as food would not be provided by TCS.

About TCS IT Wiz
Participants need to answer the preliminary written round on the pre-printed answer sheets supplied by TCS. Please carry a pen/pencil.


In each city top 6 teams will qualify for the Regional finals, based on their performance in the written preliminary round. After the corrections, prior to the finals, the quizmaster will give the answers for the prelims and announce the names of the top 6 teams and invite them on stage to participate in the Regional finals.

About the Quizmaster:



Giri Balasubramaniam, popularly known as Pickbrain has been hosting TCS IT Wiz since its inception in 1999. He heads Greycaps, Asia’s largest on stage quizzing & knowledge services Company. Well known for his distinctive style of quizzing, and a brilliant baritone voice, Giri aka Pickbrain holds seven Limca records for quizzing and has hosted more than 2200 Quiz shows across 94 destinations of India. He has also hosted quiz shows in USA, UK, Sri Lanka, Singapore, Japan and the Middle East.
